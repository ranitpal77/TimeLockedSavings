How It Works.

Everything you need to know about locking your funds on the Stellar Testnet.

1. Connect Wallet

To interact with the Time Vault, you must install and connect the Freighter Wallet extension. Ensure your wallet is switched to the Stellar Testnet and funded with test XLM.

2. Lock Funds

Navigate to the Deposit tab. Enter the amount of XLM you wish to lock, and select an unlock date using the calendar or the quick-lock slider. The Soroban smart contract will securely hold your funds. You can make multiple simultaneous deposits!

3. Withdraw Funds

Once a deposit's lock timer expires, you can withdraw it. The contract automatically loops through all your deposits and sends you the ones that have unlocked, keeping the remaining ones safely stored.
